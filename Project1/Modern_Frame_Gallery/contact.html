Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: Is admission free?
A: Yes, general admission to The Modern Frame is always free.

Q: Can I take photographs?
A: Photography is allowed for personal use only. No flash photography or commercial photography without permission.

Q: Do you offer guided tours?
A: Yes, we offer guided tours by appointment. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance.

Q: Are the artworks for sale?
A: Many pieces in our exhibitions are available for purchase. Please inquire with our gallery staff.

Q: Can I bring children?
A: Children are welcome, but we ask that they be supervised at all times.
