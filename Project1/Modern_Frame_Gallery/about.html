About The Modern Frame

Discover the story behind one of the most innovative contemporary art galleries in the region.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by visionary art collector and curator Isabella Rodriguez, The Modern Frame began as a small space with a big dream: to create a platform for contemporary artists who push boundaries and challenge conventional thinking.

What started as a modest gallery in a converted warehouse has grown into one of the most respected contemporary art spaces in the region. Our commitment to artistic excellence and innovation has remained constant throughout our journey.

Today, The Modern Frame represents over 50 emerging and established artists from around the world, showcasing works that span from traditional painting and sculpture to cutting-edge digital art and interactive installations.

Our Mission

At The Modern Frame, our mission is threefold:

  • To discover and promote emerging contemporary artists who bring fresh perspectives to the art world
  • To create meaningful connections between artists, collectors, and art enthusiasts
  • To foster dialogue about contemporary issues through the lens of visual art

We believe that art has the power to transform individuals and communities, and we are committed to making contemporary art accessible to everyone who walks through our doors.

Notable Artists

Sarah Chen

Specialty: Mixed Media & Digital Art

Years Active: 2018 - Present

Sarah Chen is a pioneering artist whose innovative approach to mixed media has earned her international recognition. Her work explores the intersection of technology and human emotion.

Maria Rodriguez

Specialty: Abstract Expressionism

Years Active: 2015 - Present

Maria Rodriguez's bold, expressive paintings capture the energy and complexity of urban life. Her work has been featured in numerous international exhibitions and private collections.

James Thompson

Specialty: Digital Art & Photography

Years Active: 2020 - Present

James Thompson pushes the boundaries of digital art, creating immersive experiences that challenge our perception of reality and virtual space.

Elena Petrov

Specialty: Contemporary Sculpture

Years Active: 2012 - Present

Elena Petrov's sculptural works explore themes of time, memory, and transformation. Her bronze and mixed media pieces are celebrated for their emotional depth and technical mastery.

Exhibition Timeline

Past Notable Exhibitions

  1. "Digital Frontiers" (2024) - A groundbreaking exhibition exploring the future of digital art and its impact on contemporary culture. Featured works from 15 international artists.
  2. "Urban Narratives" (2023) - A collection of works examining city life and urban transformation. This exhibition traveled to three major cities and received critical acclaim.
  3. "Emerging Voices" (2023) - Our annual showcase of new talent, featuring 20 up-and-coming artists from diverse backgrounds and artistic disciplines.
  4. "Boundaries & Beyond" (2022) - An exploration of artistic boundaries and the spaces between traditional and contemporary art forms.
  5. "Memory & Identity" (2022) - A deeply personal exhibition examining how artists explore themes of memory, identity, and personal history.
  6. "The Human Condition" (2021) - A powerful collection of works addressing contemporary social issues and human experiences.
  7. "Innovation in Form" (2021) - Showcasing artists who push the boundaries of traditional artistic mediums and techniques.
  8. "Global Perspectives" (2020) - Our first major international exhibition, featuring artists from 12 different countries.

Gallery Space

Our 5,000 square foot gallery space is designed to provide the perfect environment for experiencing contemporary art. The space features:

  • High ceilings with natural lighting
  • Flexible exhibition spaces
  • State-of-the-art lighting systems
  • Climate-controlled environment
  • Interactive digital displays
  • Comfortable seating areas for contemplation

The gallery is fully accessible and designed to create an intimate, engaging experience for all visitors. Our flexible layout allows us to accommodate everything from intimate solo shows to large-scale installations.
